空之合唱

Hollow Choir

三十二支无声管风琴管悬于黑森林松冠之间，由林间低频呼吸驱动 — 一座没有演奏者、没有听众席的教堂。

地点 · Site Bayerischer Wald
Bavaria · DE
时长 · Duration 14 夜
2024.10.18 — 11.01
媒介 · Medium 空气柱 · 风
无声管风琴 × 32
在场 · Attended 32 / 32 Patrons
现存 · Survives 无录音
仅一份手写日志
状态 · Status 已封存 / CLOSED

I · 构想 · Premise

森林本身是有合唱队的 — 只是大多数时候我们在它的歌之外。我们不为它写曲子，而是给它三十二根可以呼吸的金属管，让它自己挑选要不要唱、要在哪一秒唱。

整个作品没有按钮、没有时间表、没有触发器。风从拉哈河谷上溯，穿过云杉的冠层，进入管口的内腔，管子才发声。一个无风的夜里，整片森林就保持着不唱的权利。

The forest already has a choir — we are usually outside its song. We did not write music for it. We hung thirty-two breathing metal pipes among the canopy and let it choose whether and when to sing.

No buttons. No schedule. No triggers. Wind comes up from the Laha valley, threads through the spruce crowns, enters the pipe throats, and only then is there sound. On a windless night the forest keeps its right to silence.

II · 制作 · Making

管身是当地铜匠在四个月内手工切削、调校而成。每一支都有自己的基音、自己的过风阻力、自己的脾性。我们没有把它们调成和弦关系 — 让它们在风里偶然合上拍，才算是合唱。

悬挂方式是与林业工程师共同设计的：每根管子用三根可滑动钢缆固定在冠层，允许树木继续生长而不被绞断。十四夜结束后，我们花了三周拆除，没有留下任何永久结构。

The pipes were cut and tuned by hand over four months by a local Bavarian copper-smith. Each carries its own fundamental, its own air resistance, its own temperament. We did not tune them to a chord. Their occasional alignment in the wind is what we call the choir.

Suspension was engineered with the regional forestry office: three sliding cables per pipe, letting the trees keep growing without being strangled. After the fourteen nights, three weeks of dismantling. Nothing permanent left behind.

III · 进入 · Entering

Patrons 在傍晚抵达雷根的小客栈，喝过一壶杜松子茶之后，由一位向导用提灯领进林子。没有地图、没有指引牌；唯一可以依靠的是脚下那条被苔藓覆盖了一千年的小径。

步行约 40 分钟到达林心。可以坐在树根之间，可以靠着树，可以躺下。唯一被要求的是不开手机、不录像、不互相说话。两三个小时之后，向导提着灯再来接你回到客栈。

Patrons arrived at the inn in Regen at dusk, drank a pot of juniper tea, then were led into the forest by a guide carrying a lantern. No map. No signage. The only thing to follow was a path mossed over for a thousand years.

Forty minutes on foot to the heart of the woods. Sit between roots, lean against a trunk, lie down. Only requirement: no phones, no recording, no speaking to one another. Two or three hours later the guide returned with the lantern.

IV · 现存 · What Remains

没有录音，没有照片，没有视频。每位 Patron 离开时获得一页手写笔记，记录他们那一夜风的方向、气温、最强一次合鸣发生的时间。仅此而已。

第十五夜，作品被拆除。所有铜管被熔铸为一枚直径 11 厘米的环，由 Patrons 共同保管。

No recording, no photograph, no video. Each Patron received on departure a hand-written page with their night's wind direction, temperature, and the timestamp of the strongest alignment. Nothing else.

On the fifteenth night the work was dismantled. All copper was melted into a single ring, eleven centimetres across, held in common by the thirty-two.
