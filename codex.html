典籍 · Codex

这里存放着不属于任何特定 World 的记录：那些没有被写入详情页的碎片、被发现但未被采用的地点、以及由 Patrons 在离开后寄回的信件摘录。

Fragments that belong to no single World: places scouted but never used, letters Patrons sent after leaving, and quiet notes from the field.

I · 遗落的坐标 · Lost Coordinates

在决定空之合唱的森林地点之前，团队在巴伐利亚和波希米亚边境勘察了七处林区。其中一个是已经禁止进入超过三十年的军事禁区，卫星图像显示那片林子从未被砍伐过。我们最终没有选择它——不是因为无法获准进入，而是因为它太完美了。

坐标：49°12'N, 12°59'E —— 未进入，已归还给寂静。

Before settling on the forest for Hollow Choir, the team scouted seven woodlands along the Bavarian-Bohemian border. One had been a restricted military zone for over thirty years — satellite images showed it had never been logged. We did not choose it in the end, not because access was denied, but because it was too perfect.

Coordinates: 49°12'N, 12°59'E — never entered, returned to silence.

II · 来自 Patrons 的信 · Letters

一位 Patron 在参加完盐之颂歌之后半年寄来一封信。信中说她回到家之后，把那块融了一半的岩盐碎片放在冰箱里存了两个月，决定不再让它继续融化。

她在信末写道："它已经不再是石头了。它是我手心的一个形状。"

A Patron wrote to us half a year after Salt Oratorio. She said she kept the half-melted salt chip in her freezer for two months, deciding not to let it dissolve any further.

She wrote: "It is no longer a stone. It is a shape of my palm."

III · 沉默的度量 · A Measure of Silence

在枭时的八夜中，向导记录了一个数据：平均每位 Patron 从进入林子到第一次开口说话（回到小屋后）之间的沉默时长是 5 小时 28 分钟。最长的纪录是 7 小时 12 分钟——那位 Patron 在第二天告诉向导，她不是不想说话，是忘了怎么说了。

Over the eight nights of Owl Hours, the guide recorded a quiet statistic: the average time between a Patron entering the forest and their first spoken word back at the cabin was 5 hours 28 minutes. The longest was 7 hours 12 minutes — that Patron said the next day she had not forgotten how to speak; she had forgotten that she could.

IV · 未被采用的名称 · Rejected Titles

每一项 World 在最终命名之前都有过至少二十个被放弃的方案。以下是几个我们最喜欢的遗珠：

· 空之合唱最初叫《气管》（Windpipes）
· 潮汐图谱曾叫《大西洋的呼吸》（The Atlantic's Breath）
· 盐之颂歌在第一天就叫盐之颂歌——它是唯一没有被改过的。
· 织梦的内部代号是《线头》（Thread）。

Every World had at least twenty rejected titles before its final name. A few favourites:

· Hollow Choir was first called Windpipes.
· Tide Atlas was nearly The Atlantic's Breath.
· Salt Oratorio was Salt Oratorio from day one — the only one never changed.
· The Loom's internal code was Thread.
