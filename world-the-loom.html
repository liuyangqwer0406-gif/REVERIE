I · 构想 · Premise

一座废弃的比利牛斯石场，三百米长的岩壁之间。我们不修复它、不改建它、不在里面放任何东西 — 我们只是在两面岩壁之间拉一根线，然后慢慢编织。让羊毛在石头之间生长，让时间成为唯一的材料。

An abandoned Pyrenean quarry, three hundred metres between its rock walls. We do not restore it, convert it, or place anything inside — we simply pull a thread between two walls and weave, slowly. Let wool grow between stone. Let time be the only material.

II · 制作 · Making

织物由三位织工用传统比利牛斯羊毛手工编织，每月推进约十米。不使用机器、不使用化学染料 — 羊毛来自石场周边牧民的羊群，颜色就是羊本身的颜色：灰白、深褐、焦黑。编织从石场一端开始，向另一端推进，像一条缓慢生长的河流。

The textile is hand-woven by three artisans using traditional Pyrenean wool, advancing roughly ten metres per month. No machinery, no chemical dyes — the wool comes from local shepherds' flocks; the colours are the sheep's own: ash-white, deep brown, scorched black. Weaving begins at one end of the quarry and advances toward the other, like a river growing slowly.

III · 进入 · Entering

Patrons 每月可来一次，由向导带入石场观看织物的推进。每次到访，织物比上个月长了十米左右。没有演出、没有讲解 — 只有你和一条正在生长的布。可以触摸织物的边缘，可以坐在石场底部仰望岩壁之间的羊毛天空。完工之夜（2026 年 2 月），所有 Patrons 被邀请回到石场，观看织物被点燃。

Patrons may visit once a month, guided into the quarry to watch the textile advance. Each visit, it is roughly ten metres longer than the month before. No performance, no narration — only you and a cloth that is growing. You may touch its edge, sit at the bottom of the quarry and look up at a wool sky between rock walls. On the final night (February 2026), all Patrons are invited back to watch it burn.

IV · 现存 · What Remains

织物仍在石场中。此刻它大约已经推进了七个月，长度约七十米。完工之夜它将被点燃 — 火焰会从一端烧到另一端，耗时约四十分钟。燃烧结束后，石场恢复原状，只剩灰烬和石头之间残留的羊毛焦痕。每位 Patrons 将获得一小块从织物边缘剪下的样片。

The textile is still in the quarry. Right now it has been advancing for about seven months, roughly seventy metres long. On the final night it will be set alight — the flame will travel from one end to the other in about forty minutes. After the burn the quarry returns to its original state: only ash and scorch marks where wool met stone. Each Patron will receive a small swatch cut from the textile's edge.
