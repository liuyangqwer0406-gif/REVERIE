I · 构想 · Premise

涨潮时海蚀洞被海水完全淹没，壁画消失在水面之下。退潮时，只有半小时窗口，水印壁画从岩壁上缓缓浮现。我们不控制潮汐，不建造堤坝，不安装水泵 — 让大西洋自己决定什么时候展示、什么时候收回。

At high tide the sea cave submerges entirely; the mural vanishes beneath the water. At low tide there is a thirty-minute window during which the watermark painting emerges from the rock. We do not control the tide, build dams, or install pumps — we let the Atlantic decide when to show and when to take back.

II · 制作 · Making

壁画由法罗群岛本地艺术家用传统矿物颜料直接绘于海蚀洞内壁。颜料配方经过三年测试，能在盐水中存活而不伤害岩面。六周驻地期间，每天只有退潮的半小时可以工作。壁画完成后不密封、不保护 — 它的寿命由海洋决定。

The mural was painted directly onto the cave's interior walls by a local Faroese artist using traditional mineral pigments. The formula survived three years of testing to endure saltwater without harming the rock. During the six-week residency, only the half-hour low-tide window allowed work. After completion, no sealant, no protection — the ocean decides its lifespan.

III · 进入 · Entering

Patrons 乘渔船抵达瓦格尔岛，在退潮前 15 分钟由向导带入洞穴。进入时洞内完全黑暗，海水齐膝。退潮开始后，壁画从水面以下逐渐显露，如同一张地图从水底浮起。三十分钟后潮水返回，壁画再次消失。

Patrons reach Vágar by fishing boat and are led into the cave fifteen minutes before low tide. Inside, total darkness, knee-deep water. As the tide recedes, the mural surfaces from below — like a map rising from the seabed. Thirty minutes later the water returns and the painting disappears again.

IV · 现存 · What Remains

壁画不可搬运，不可复制，不可拍照（洞内光线不足以成像）。每位 Patrons 获得一块从洞外海滩捡拾的石头，上面刻有他们到访那天的潮汐时间。壁画本身仍在洞中 — 此刻可能在水下，也可能刚刚露出。

The mural cannot be moved, copied, or photographed (insufficient light inside the cave). Each Patron receives a stone from the beach outside, engraved with the tide times of their visit. The painting itself remains in the cave — right now it may be underwater, or it may have just emerged.
